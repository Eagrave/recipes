Ham and Mushroom Scrambled Egg

Ingredients

  • 4 cloves garlic
  • 1 pc onion
  • 0.5pc Red bell pepper
  • 0.5 cup ham
  • 0.5 cup canned mushroom
  • 2 tablespoon butter
  • 1 sachet MAGGi magic sarap 8g
  • 6pcs Egg
  • 1 tablespoon Parsley
  1. Sauté garlic, onion, bell pepper, ham and mushroom in butter. Season with ½ sachet of MAGGI® Magic Sarap®.
  2. Beat eggs and season with remaining ½ sachet of MAGGI® Magic Sarap®.
  3. Pour beaten egg and gently stir until barely set. Stir in parsley. Transfer on a serving plate and serve.
